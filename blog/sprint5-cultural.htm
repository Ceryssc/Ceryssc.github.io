Cultural Five

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

I have learnt how important it is to consciously listen, especially for growing trust and sincerity. I learned about growth mindset and how important it is to remember “I just haven’t learnt it, yet”, when I’m struggling with something.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

I actually enjoyed it and it challenged me to think more than I thought it would about others and the world around me.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Meditation was quite challenging, however I know this is something that can be learnt and developed through practice.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

We spend so much time focusing on core learning and understanding emotional intelligence because these skills helps with out concentration, our ability to communicate well with others and to become more aware of our own needs and those of others.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer.

I don’t think it is a waste of time because core learning provides of us with the essential skills we need to succeed and are just as important as being a good programmer
