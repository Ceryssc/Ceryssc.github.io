What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self awareness is made up three emotional competencies, and the first being emotion awareness. This is recognising one’s emotions and their effects. The second one being accurate self-assessment: knowing one’s strengths and limits. The third is self confidence: This is a strong sense of one’s self-worth and capabilities. Self awareness and mindfulness are closely related and both are trainable.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

That we are not are our emotions and that they should not define us. Mindfulness practice helps us shift our emotions from being existential (“I am”) to experiential (“I feel”). I really liked the traditional metaphor “Thoughts and emotions are like clouds—some beautiful, some dark—while our core being is like the sky. Clouds are not the sky; they are phenomena in the sky that come and go”. This is essentially saying that thoughts and emotions are a phenomena in the mind and body that come and go, but they are not what we are, and what defines us.
