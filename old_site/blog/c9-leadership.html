How does the SCARF model apply to your Phase 0 experience so far? How will it apply to bootcamp?

The SCARF model is made up of five domains: Status, Certainty, Autonomy, Relatedness and Fairness. During my Phase 0 experience I have found there to be a status reward, when comparing myself to before the start of Phase 0. I know more than when I started.

There has been less certainty at times, especially when I feel blocked, about how well I am progressing and will I have understood enough for bootcamp.

At bootcamp I think the SCARF model will apply more due to the cohort being together in person, and relatedness and fairness will take bigger roles. Bootcamp should hopefully bring more certainty and status also
